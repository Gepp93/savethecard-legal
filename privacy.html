Privacy Policy – SaveTheCard Last updated: [29.12.2025] SaveTheCard (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the SaveTheCard mobile application. Information We Collect SaveTheCard may collect the following information: Account Information Email address (used for authentication) Optional display name User Content Gift card images you choose to upload or scan Gift card details such as merchant name, balance, and expiration date Device Permissions Camera access (to scan gift cards) Photo library access (to select gift card images) Notifications (to send expiration reminders) Subscription Information Subscription status and entitlement (handled securely by Apple via StoreKit) We do not store or process payment details How We Use Your Information We use your information to: Provide and improve app functionality Store and display your gift cards Send reminder notifications Manage subscriptions and app access Provide customer support Data Storage Your data may be stored: Locally on your device Securely via third-party services such as Firebase (authentication and data storage) We take reasonable steps to protect your data. Third-Party Services SaveTheCard uses third-party services including: Apple (Sign in with Apple, App Store purchases) Firebase (authentication and data storage) These services have their own privacy policies. Data Retention You may delete your account at any time. When you do: Your account data is permanently deleted Associated stored data is removed Your Rights You may: Access your data Request deletion of your account Control permissions via device settings Children’s Privacy SaveTheCard is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
